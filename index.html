About Us

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is a dynamic, national non-profit organization dedicated to advancing technical education in India. With a focus on empowering teachers and nurturing students' personalities, ISTE plays a vital role in shaping our country's technical education system. As the sole national association of educators in Engineering and Technology, ISTE actively collaborates with the government on various initiatives. Its strength lies in its extensive network of technical education institutions across the nation.
